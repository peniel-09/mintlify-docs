## Tools and Accessories
<CardGroup col={2}>
    <Card
        title="Breadboard Jumper Wires"
        icon="jumper-wires"
    >
        ![Breadboard Jumper Wires](/images/jumper.jpg)
        Breadboard jumper wires are used to connect the various components on the breadboard, allowing for easy circuit assembly and modification.
    </Card>
    <Card

    <Card

    <Card
        title="Wire Cutters and Pliers"
        icon="wire-cutters" 
    >
        ![Wire Cutters and Pliers](/images/pince.webp)

        the assembly of the robot, making it easier to work with small components and connections.
    </Card>
